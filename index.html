Rosemary Cottage Care Limited is a locally owned, run and focused company, with the needs of the island at its heart.

Rosemary Cottage is a specialist registered residential care home providing medium and long term residential care to those living with drug and alcohol related issues such as Wernicke-Korsakoff's syndrome and other mental health illness. The home caters for up to 7 residents.

We are partnered with SMART Recovery who advocate self-empowering skills and supporting each other in recovery.

Our services

  • Psychosocial drug & alcohol support (including mutual aid & relapse prevention work)
  • Reablement and skills development
  • Nutritional and diet support
  • Physical and emotional health support
  • Medication support
  • Practical and social support
  • Personal care assistance where needed
  • 24 Hour Care

Services are not provided to individuals who continue to use drugs or alcohol

Our philosophy

We offer skilled and professional care to enable the individuals living at Rosemary Cottage to achieve their optimum state of health and well-being.

We respect and encourage the individual uniqueness of residents, staff and visitors, and treat everyone with dignity and respect at all times. We encourage independence and the need for personal fulfilment, offering individualised options for meaningful activities to satisfy the needs of the residents and staff.

Assessment process

Rosemary Cottage strives to help residents to achieve greater independence, by providing a safe home and ongoing psychosocial support that is unique to each individual.

Careful assessment of each person admitted to the home is carried out prior to admission. This assessment, alongside any assessment completed by a referring professional and involvement from the resident, is then used to put a package of care and support in place that will enable each person to be as independent as possible.

We encourage the development of former skills and interests, as well as providing opportunities for new experiences to enrich residents lives. Additionally, by providing relapse prevention support in a safe and secure environment we intend to help our residents to achieve personal aspirations and maximise their abilities. The team at Rosemary Cottage encourage links with a residents family; often lost when lives are disrupted by drug or alcohol abuse.
